3 / Data scarcity

The core defect is missing operating history

A pure optimizer assumes clean inputs. A pure model wants history. Greek standalone batteries have neither at the asset level yet.

Scarce battery telemetry

No long local record of SoC paths, degradation, BMS behavior, downtime, or dispatch response.

Fragmented external signals

HEnEx prices, IPTO/ENTSO-E data, weather, gas, carbon, and shock context live in different formats.

Uncertain asset specs

Headline MW/MWh does not equal usable AC dispatchable energy.

Changing market rules

Trial operation, imbalance exposure, 15-minute MTUs, and support obligations change what a profitable plan means.
